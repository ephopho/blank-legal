Privacy Policy · Last updated 20 June 2026

The short version

  • Blank works entirely on your device.
  • Your messages, passwords, and audio files are never uploaded to us or anyone else.
  • We do not collect personal information, run analytics, or track you.

Information we do not collect

Blank has no accounts and no login. We do not collect your name, email, contacts, location, messages, passwords, or the audio you choose. Messages are encrypted and hidden locally; the resulting file only ever leaves your device if you choose to share it.

Purchases

Blank Pro is a one-time in-app purchase processed by Apple. We use RevenueCat to manage entitlements (to know whether Pro is unlocked). RevenueCat receives a randomly generated, anonymous app-user identifier and the purchase receipt — not your name or contact details. This is used solely to deliver the feature you bought and to support “Restore purchase.”

  • Apple’s privacy practices: apple.com/legal/privacy
  • RevenueCat’s privacy policy: revenuecat.com/privacy

Children

Blank does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the “Last updated” date above will change accordingly.
